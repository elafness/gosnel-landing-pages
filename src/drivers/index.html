Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements?

You must be 21+ years old, have a valid UAE driving license, and own or have access to a reliable vehicle (motorcycle, car, or van).

How much can I earn?

Most drivers earn AED 1,500-3,000 per week depending on hours worked and delivery volume. Top performers earn even more.

When do I get paid?

Earnings are transferred daily to your bank account. You can also request instant withdrawal anytime with zero fees.

Do I need my own vehicle?

Yes, you'll need your own vehicle. We provide insurance coverage while you're delivering.
