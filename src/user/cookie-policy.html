Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2024

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and improving our service.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They include:

  • Session management
  • Authentication
  • Security features

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience by remembering:

  • Your language preferences
  • Previous orders and preferences
  • Location settings

Analytics Cookies

We use these to understand how you use our website:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Popular features and content
  • Performance and error tracking

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Most browsers allow you to delete cookies from your computer
  • You can configure your browser to reject cookies
  • You can set your browser to notify you when cookies are being used
  • Some browsers offer private browsing modes that don't store cookies

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality and your user experience.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Payment processors for secure transactions
  • Analytics services to improve our website
  • Customer support tools

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
