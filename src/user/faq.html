Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GoSnel

How do I order a meal?

Simply chat with our AI assistant and describe what you're craving or your mood. The AI will suggest perfect meals for you and help you place an order with local restaurants.

Is GoSnel free to use?

Yes! GoSnel is completely free for customers. You only pay for the food you order. There are no subscription fees, hidden charges, or delivery fees from our side.

How does GoSnel know my preferences?

Our AI learns from your conversations, order history, and feedback. The more you chat with GoSnel, the better it becomes at understanding your taste, dietary restrictions, and budget preferences.

Which areas do you serve?

We currently serve Ajman, UAE, with plans to expand to other emirates soon. Sign up to be notified when we launch in your area.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your order through the chat interface within a reasonable time frame before the restaurant starts preparing your meal.

Do you support dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! GoSnel supports vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, keto, halal, and other dietary preferences. Just tell our AI about your dietary needs and it will filter recommendations accordingly.

Is there a mobile app?

A mobile app is coming soon! For now, you can use GoSnel through your web browser on any device. The experience is fully optimized for mobile.

Our support team is here to help!
