Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last updated: December 8, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to GoSnel ("we," "our," or "us"). We are a food delivery platform dedicated to connecting users with the best local restaurants and vendors across the United Arab Emirates, including Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Umm Al Quwain, Ras Al Khaimah, and Fujairah.

We value your trust. This document outlines how we handle your data and the terms that govern your use of our app and services. By using GoSnel, you agree to these terms.

2. Privacy Policy

2.1 Information We Collect

To provide you with seamless delivery, we collect the following types of data:

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Data: Your Full Name, Email Address, and Phone Number (for OTP verification and account security).
  • Delivery Details: Your Delivery Address and optional instructions (e.g., apartment number, gate codes) to ensure your food arrives at the right place.
  • Preferences: Your meal preferences, dietary restrictions, allergies, and profile photo (optional).
  • Communications: Chat history with our support team or AI assistant.

B. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Location Data: We collect GPS data to show you nearby restaurants and track deliveries. You can control this via your device settings.
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, and operating system for security and optimization.
  • Usage Data: Order history, favorite restaurants, and app interaction logs.

C. Financial Information

We take payment security seriously. GoSnel does NOT store your full credit card numbers, CVV codes, or expiration dates. All payments are processed securely by Stripe, a globally recognized payment processor.

2.2 App Permissions

To function correctly, the GoSnel app may request the following permissions on your device:

  • Location: Used to identify your delivery address and show nearby vendors.
  • Camera/Photos: Used for updating your profile picture or, for drivers, uploading required documentation.
  • Notifications: Used to send real-time updates about your order status and delivery arrival.

2.3 How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly to:

  1. Fulfill Service: Process orders, manage payments, and coordinate deliveries.
  2. Communication: Send order confirmations, receipts, and important service updates.
  3. Support & Safety: Provide customer support, prevent fraud, and comply with UAE legal obligations.
  4. Improvement: Enhance our AI recommendations and app performance.

2.4 Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with partners necessary to fulfill our service:

  • Service Providers: Stripe (Payments), Supabase (Database), OpenAI (AI Chat), Google (Maps), and Cloudflare (Security).
  • Order Fulfillment: Restaurants receive your order details; Drivers receive your delivery location and contact info.
  • Legal: Law enforcement agencies if legally required.

2.5 Data Retention & Deletion (Your Rights)

Retention: We keep order and payment records for 7 years to comply with tax and legal regulations. Chat logs are retained for 90 days.

Account Deletion (Right to Erasure): You have the right to delete your account at any time.

  • In-App: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account.
  • By Request: Email us at privacy@gosnel.com.
  • Effect: Upon deletion request, your account info is removed within 30 days, though legal/financial records may be retained as required by law.

2.6 Children's Privacy

GoSnel is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
