1. Introduction

Welcome to GoSnel ("we," "our," "us"). GoSnel is an AI-powered meal recommendation and discovery platform that connects users with local restaurants and vendors in the United Arab Emirates.

By using GoSnel's website, mobile applications, or any of our services, you agree to be bound by these terms and policies.

Service Coverage: United Arab Emirates (Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Umm Al Quwain, Ras Al Khaimah, Fujairah)

2. Privacy Policy

2.1 Information We Collect

Personal Information (Users)

We collect the following information when you create an account or use our services:

  • Full Name - Account identification, delivery (Required)
  • Email Address - Account login, notifications, receipts (Required)
  • Phone Number - Order coordination, OTP verification (Required)
  • Delivery Address - Order fulfillment (Required)
  • GPS Location - Accurate delivery, nearby restaurants (Optional)
  • Profile Photo - Account personalization (Optional)
  • Preferred Language - Service localization (EN/AR) (Optional)

Order & Preference Data

  • Meal preferences (dietary restrictions, allergies)
  • Delivery notes (apartment number, gate codes)
  • Order history and favorite restaurants
  • Budget preferences
  • Chat conversations with our AI assistant

Payment Information

We use Stripe as our payment processor. GoSnel does NOT store:

  • Full credit/debit card numbers
  • CVV/CVC codes
  • Card expiration dates

Stripe securely processes and stores all payment information.

2.2 How We Use Your Information

  • Process and deliver your orders - Contract performance
  • Send order confirmations and updates - Contract performance
  • Process payments securely - Contract performance
  • Provide customer support - Legitimate interest
  • Improve our AI recommendations - Legitimate interest
  • Send promotional offers (with consent) - Consent
  • Comply with legal obligations - Legal requirement
  • Prevent fraud and abuse - Legitimate interest

2.3 Information Sharing

We share your information with:

Service Providers:

  • Stripe - Payment processing
  • Supabase - Database hosting (PostgreSQL)
  • OpenAI - AI chat assistance (anonymized conversations)
  • Google - Maps and location services
  • Cloudflare - Website hosting and security

Third Parties:

  • Restaurants/Vendors (order details for fulfillment)
  • Delivery drivers (delivery address and contact)
  • Law enforcement (when legally required)

We NEVER sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

2.4 Your Rights

  • Access - Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification - Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure - Delete your account and data
  • Portability - Export your data
  • Restriction - Limit processing of your data
  • Objection - Object to marketing communications

4. Cookie Policy

4.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better experience by remembering your preferences and improving our service.

4.2 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Security features and fraud prevention
  • Payment processing security

Functional Cookies

Enhance your experience by remembering:

  • Language preferences and settings
  • Previous orders and preferences
  • Location and timezone settings

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand website usage:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Popular features and content
  • Performance and error tracking

4.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to delete cookies, reject them, or receive notifications when they're used. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

4.4 Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Payment processors for secure transactions
  • Analytics services to improve our website
  • Customer support tools

5. Vendor Terms & Conditions

5.1 Vendor Agreement Overview

This agreement governs your use of GoSnel as a restaurant vendor partner. By registering, you agree to be bound by these terms.

5.2 Account Registration & Management

  • Provide accurate and complete business information
  • Maintain account security and confidentiality
  • Do not operate multiple accounts without approval
  • Maintain valid health permits and business licenses

5.3 Commission & Payment Terms

  • Commission: GoSnel retains a percentage of each order
  • Settlements: Weekly payouts to registered bank account
  • Fees: Additional fees may apply for refunds or chargebacks
  • Disputes: May dispute charges within 30 days

5.4 Order Management Responsibilities

Vendor partners agree to:

  • Respond to orders within specified timeframe (typically 2 minutes)
  • Prepare food according to customer specifications
  • Maintain food safety standards and hygiene
  • Label items with special requirements (allergens, etc.)
  • Communicate delays or issues immediately

5.5 Quality Standards & Ratings

GoSnel monitors vendor performance through customer ratings, order times, complaint rates, and compliance audits. Vendors with consistently low performance may face suspension or termination.

5.6 Termination

Either party may terminate with 30 days notice. GoSnel may terminate immediately for health violations, fraudulent activity, or repeated quality breaches.

5.7 Liability & Disclaimers

Vendors are solely responsible for food quality, safety, and preparation. GoSnel is not liable for allergic reactions or food poisoning. Maximum liability is limited to commission fees paid in the last 30 days.
